About Me

Hello, my name is Corey Lanier, and I am an American multilingual (English, Mandarin, Korean, Japanese, Esperanto) web developer based in Toronto. I am a graduate of HackerYou's Web Development Immersive with a passion for competitive gaming, food, and writing.

I have a deep passion for writing clean Javascript, and React is one of my favourite frameworks to work in. I also enjoy learning about new technologies and frameworks that can help make my designs more efficient and lively.

Before starting my career in tech, I lived in China and South Korea for 5 years teaching English as a Second Language. Alongside knowledge and skills, I bring curiosity, enthusiasm, and a whole lot of care to everything I do.
